Q: What happens if I ignore CRA payroll debt?
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A: If you ignore CRA payroll debt, the CRA will contact your bank directly with requirement to pay letters (FAQ 7 - Payroll Debt and Financing Ability, 00:00 → 01:35). Additionally, by the time you are one year behind in payroll, you can expect harassing phone calls and letters to start (FAQ 1 - Consequences of Unpaid Payroll Deductions, 02:35 → 03:33, and FAQ 2 - Behind on Payroll Source Deduction Payments, 00:00 → 01:41). The director of a corporation will also be pursued (FAQ 2 - Behind on Payroll Source Deduction Payments, 01:16 → 03:00).
